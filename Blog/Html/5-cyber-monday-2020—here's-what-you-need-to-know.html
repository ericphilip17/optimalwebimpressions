Cyber Monday is days away - Here Is everything you need to know


It is here again—in a couple of days!

A special day to say goodbye to November and welcome December in style!

Yes, a day where every brand goes all out to offer you super and executive deals, better than those of black Fridays!

How better could we culminate iNovember?

So let’s dive right in and get a couple of insights about Cyber Monday.


What and When is Cyber Monday 2020?


According to the New York Times (2005), the name “Cyber Monday” came from the observation that most Americans would spend the weekend after Thanksgiving day doing window shopping.

On the incoming Monday, they would flock to the online stores to order their best deals.

And because Thanksgiving day happens the fourth Thursday of every November, Cyber Monday is usually four days later.

It’s also the first Monday after the last black Friday.

So in 2020, it’s a no-brainer—Cyber Monday is happening this November 30th.

5 Top stores to have on your radar for Cyber Monday 2020

8 Top tips for shopping on Cyber Monday 2020?

• Check out your favorite store’s sales in their recent years and try predicting what deals they may offer
• Bookmark the brands you love or create a dedicated folder to help you single out things as Cyber Monday approaches
• Follow the brands on their social media platforms to get notified when they have deals you may love to partake
• Take advantage of your brand’s newsletters—sign up to keep learning about their Cyber Monday deals
• Be the Cyber Monday early bird—create your gifts list as early as possible to avoid impulse buying and concentrate on what’s more important to you and your family or friends
• Research about brands’ policies—for instance, shipping, return, and refund policies
• Keep stocking up gift cards to enjoy even better discounts when Cyber Monday.
• Use tools like SlickDeals to follow brands you are interested in and get notified any time they launch Cyber Monday discounts
