How to stream MLB games FROM EVERYWHERE


Whether you are a baseball player or just a passionate fan, its obvious one of your favorite gaming seasons is the Major League Baseball (MLB). Right from the kick-off to the final hit, every game has a fun part and lots of lessons to learn—they all leave a real spectacle. Each game you watch gets you longing for more.


However, it’s unfortunate that you cannot watch MLB outside the US or Canada. So if you traveled outside the country before or during the game, most likely, you are wondering how you’ll stream normally, right? Well, you really have no reason to worry—this post will enlighten you on the most efficient ways to stream MLB games from anywhere around the world.


But before that;


Where Are MLB Games Broadcasted?


MLB.TV

MLB.TV is the official platform that broadcasts MLB games in the US and Canada. The platform is responsive enough to support streaming from any device. However, some districts do experience blackouts once in a while.


ESPN

In case you are limited to access MLB.TV, ESPN could be the next alternative you need. Even though its games keep changing every year, it’s still a great option, especially for the high-quality videos it broadcasts. Again, it’s also limited to viewing in the US and Canada.


TBS

TBS works in conjunction with an array of other broadcasters around the world. Just like ESPN, it streams a limited number of games—but not live. It’s a decent option if you want to watch a game you missed.


So, how do you stream outside the US and Canada?

As you may have noted, MLB.TV is the most convenient platform for streaming baseball. But now that it is only limited to the US and Canada, you need an alternative to bypass the geolocation restrictions. Note that the blocking systems identify your location using the Internet Protocol (IP) address of your device—which varies from one country to another.


But thanks to Virtual Private Networks (VPN) software—you can easily change your IP address to that of the US or Canada and stream normally. Better yet, VPNs like Surfshark VPN help you avoid disappointments of sports blackouts because you have a variety of options if one channel fails.


How to Use a VPN to Watch MLB Games on MLB.tv


Choose a reliable VPN provider

Before opting for one, ensure that it operates at high speeds and that its servers are within the locality of the team you wish to watch. High speeds help you stream with minimal hiccups and also avoid any blackouts.


Stage the program/app

Download and install the software on your device. Then launch the software and select your preferred location. Connect the VPN to the US if the game is playing live there, or Canada—if appropriate. With that, you are now in your preferred destination, virtually.


Subscribe on MLB.TV

On your browser, head over to MLB.TV then subscribe. You will be receiving notifications for the games you are interested in. From there, you can log in and stream comfortably from anywhere in the world.


Why Use a VPN to Watch MLB Games?


Feel free to travel anywhere

A good number of MLB fans in the US and Canada feel betrayed by the fact that they cannot stream when they go outside the country. Fortunately, you don’t have to cancel your meetings because of geo-restrictions. Connect to an efficient VPN and “go back home”.


Get past blackouts seamlessly

We are in 2020—with so many exciting baseball games an ardent fan doesn’t want to miss. However, blackouts are still here. Actually, even some local districts get limited. And the only solution is masking your IP address using a VPN.


Browse securely

Besides accessing MLB games, VPNs encrypt your activities on the internet to allow you to browse anonymously. So, whether you want to stream anonymously or keep spies off your online engagements, you will definitely have to hide your IP address with a VPN.


3 Tips and Tricks for Easy Streaming When Using a VPN


Clear Browser Cookies

If you had already logged into MLB.TV before launching the VPN, be sure to refresh the page once you connected. Refreshing the page clears your browser cookies that contain the geolocation details.


Re-install the MLB app on Smart TV

Sometimes when using VPNs on a Smart TV, the MLB app malfunctions. In that case, one of the best tips is to uninstall the app, refresh the device, then install it again. That restores everything in place to avoid frequent hiccups.


You don’t need another MLB account

Perhaps you are wondering, “Now that I have changed my IP address, do I need a fresh MLB account?” A quick answer is—NO. Just log in to MLB.TV using your usual account and stream everything from there.


The Bottom Line

Regardless of where you live, there is no point of missing your favorite MLB games because your location is blacked out. But because their performance varies from one to another, it is imperative to research and subscribe to the best VPN provider for the best experience.
